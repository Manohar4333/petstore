Frequetly asked questions


We recommend selecting pet food based on your pet’s breed, age, size, and specific dietary needs. Look for high-quality ingredients and avoid artificial additives. If you're unsure, consult with your vet or check out our blog on Choosing the Right Food for Your Pet for more detailed advice.

Common signs of health issues include changes in appetite, unusual behavior, vomiting, diarrhea, excessive scratching, or lethargy. If you notice any of these symptoms, it’s best to consult your vet. For more information, read our blog on Recognizing Common Health Issues in Pets.

Grooming frequency depends on your pet's breed and coat type. Dogs with long hair may need grooming every week, while short-haired breeds can go longer between grooming sessions. Cats typically groom themselves, but regular brushing is still important. Visit our blog on Grooming Tips for a Healthy, Happy Pet for more detailed grooming advice.
